Privacy Policy for OpenLoot 2FA Helper

Last Updated: January 6, 2025

1. Introduction

OpenLoot 2FA Helper is a Chrome extension designed to help users submit 2FA verification codes for OpenLoot. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling any information we collect with care and transparency.

2. Information Collection

Our extension collects and stores the following limited information temporarily:

  • Session ID: A temporary identifier used for API authentication
  • Device ID: A temporary identifier used for API authentication
  • 2FA verification status and timer information

3. How We Use Information

The collected information is used solely for:

  • Facilitating 2FA verification with OpenLoot's API
  • Maintaining the verification status during your session

4. Data Storage and Security

All information is:

  • Stored locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local
  • Never uploaded to any external servers
  • Never shared with any third parties
  • Automatically cleared when your session ends
  • Transmitted securely using HTTPS when communicating with OpenLoot's API

5. Third-Party Access

We do not:

  • Sell or rent your information to third parties
  • Share your information with any third parties
  • Use your information for any purpose other than 2FA verification

6. Data Retention

All stored information is temporary and is automatically cleared when:

  • Your session ends
  • You close your browser
  • You manually reset the 2FA verification

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear stored data at any time by clicking the "Reset Verification" button
  • Uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove all stored data

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
